# FutureOS

**FutureOS is a desktop AI agent workspace.** Instead of just chatting and getting an answer, you get an assistant whose work you can *watch and check*: what it read, what commands it ran, which files changed, what it's waiting for your approval on, and how to pick the work back up later.

It's built for people pushing real, multi-step tasks forward — software work, research, data analysis, writing, report generation, debugging — all in one place.

## What you can do

- **Have a conversation** with an AI agent that streams its thinking, runs tools, and shows its work.
- **Start a quick Chat**, or open a **Workspace** tied to a folder on your computer to work on real files.
- **Stay in control** — the agent pauses and asks for your approval before anything risky.
- **Check the work** — see background tasks, file changes, and the files and results it produced.
- **Use skills** — built-in capability packs for research, documents, images, the web, and more.
